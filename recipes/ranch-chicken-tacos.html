Ranch Chicken Tacos Recipe

Season chicken breasts with ranch seasoning and smoked paprika, then cook until fully done and browned. Shred the chicken and prepare a cilantro lime ranch sauce by blending ranch dressing, fresh cilantro, lime juice, and garlic powder.

Warm tortillas and fill them with the shredded chicken. Drizzle the cilantro lime ranch sauce over the chicken and add toppings like shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and sliced avocado for a quick, flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • Cooked chicken, shredded
  • Taco seasoning
  • Ranch dressing
  • Taco shells
  • Shredded cheddar cheese

    • Steps to prepare Ranch Chicken Tacos

    1. Shred cooked chicken and mix with taco seasoning.
    2. Stir in ranch dressing to the seasoned chicken.
    3. Heat the taco shells.
    4. Fill taco shells with the chicken mixture and top with cheese.
    5. Add any additional toppings like lettuce or tomatoes, and serve.
