Chicken Noodles Salad Recipe

Chicken noodle salad is a refreshing dish perfect for a light meal. It combines tender chicken, crisp vegetables, and noodles with a zesty dressing.

Simply cook the noodles, grill the chicken, mix with vegetables, and toss with dressing. Enjoy immediately!

Ingredients

  • Coodles
  • Chicken breasts
  • Carrots
  • Bell peppers
  • Soy sauce

Steps for preparing chicken noodles salad

  1. Cook the noodles until tender and cool them.
  2. Grill or saut√© the chicken breasts and slice them.
  3. Mix the noodles, chicken, and fresh vegetables in a bowl.
  4. Add chopped herbs for extra flavor.
  5. Toss with a simple dressing and serve immediately. Enjoy!
