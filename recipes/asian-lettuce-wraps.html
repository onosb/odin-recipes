Asian Lettuce Wraps

Asian lettuce wraps feature a savory filling of ground meat, finely chopped vegetables, and a flavorful sauce made from soy sauce, hoisin sauce, garlic, and ginger. Cook the meat until browned, add the vegetables and sauce, and let everything simmer until well combined.

To assemble, use large, crisp lettuce leaves like Boston Bibb or butter lettuce. Spoon the filling into the center of each leaf, wrap it like a burrito, and enjoy the fresh, crunchy lettuce paired with the rich, savory filling.

Ingredients

  • Ground meat (chicken, beef, or turkey)
  • Soy sauce
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Garlic
  • Lettuce leaves (Boston Bibb or butter lettuce)

Steps to prepare Asian Lettuce Wraps

  1. Brown ground meat (chicken, beef, or turkey) in a pan.
  2. Mix soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and minced garlic
  3. Add finely chopped vegetables to the meat, then pour in the sauce and simmer.
  4. Wash and dry large lettuce leaves (Boston Bibb or butter lettuce).
  5. Spoon the filling into the center of each lettuce leaf, wrap, and serve.
